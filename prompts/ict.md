## Core Concepts (To Be Integrated Later)

### 1. Fair Value Gaps (FVG)
- 3-candle pattern where candles don't overlap
- Bullish FVG: high[2] > low[0]
- Bearish FVG: low[2] < high[0]
- Used as entry zones and profit targets

### 2. Market Structure Shift (MSS)
- Break of Structure (BOS): Price breaks swing high/low
- Change of Character (CHoCH): Trend change signal

### 3. Order Blocks
- Last candle before strong directional move
- Areas where institutions placed orders

### 4. Two-Strike Rule
- Two failed attempts at a level
- Indicates strong support/resistance

### 5. Liquidity Pools
- Daily highs/lows
- Previous day ranges
- Equal highs/lows

### 6. Kill Zones
- New York: 7:00 AM - 10:00 AM EST
- London: 2:00 AM - 5:00 AM EST
- Avoid: Lunch 11:30 AM - 1:00 PM EST
