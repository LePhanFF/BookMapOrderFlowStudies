## Why Adding Strategies Hurts

### The Volume vs Safety Tradeoff

We tested every available strategy combination:

| Config | Trades | WR | BT$/Mo | Eval% | Funded Surv | MC$/Mo |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Core 5 only | 29 | 75.9% | $1,370 | 100% | 100% | $870 |
| + EMA Trend Follow | 80 | 52.5% | $2,506 | 93% | **25%** | $303 |
| + LiqSweep + EMA | 83 | 51.8% | $2,417 | ~85% | ~15% | ~$100 |
| + EMA + ORB | 95 | 48.4% | $1,886 | ~75% | ~5% | ~-$30 |
| + ALL 3 extras | 98 | 48.0% | $1,797 | ~70% | ~3% | ~-$40 |

**Pattern:** Every extra strategy adds trades but drops WR below 55%, which is catastrophic for prop firm survival. The $4,500 trailing DD is extremely unforgiving of losing streaks.

### Why 52% WR Kills Prop Accounts

At 52.5% WR with 1.29 trades/session:
- **Expected losing streak of 5+:** happens ~2.4x per year
- Each 5-day losing streak costs ~$1,500–$2,000
- Two such streaks within 30 days = $3,000–$4,000 drawdown → blown

At 73.1% WR with 0.42 trades/session:
- **Expected losing streak of 5+:** happens 0.01x per year (almost never)
- Each losing trade is smaller (higher avg win from concentrated sizing)
- The account steadily compounds with rare, small pullbacks

### The Relaxed Strategy Variants

We also tested relaxed versions of every strategy:

| Relaxation | Effect |
|---|---|
| Lower confidence thresholds (0.375→0.25) | Added ~5 trades, dropped WR by 10% → net negative |
| 1/3 quality gate (was 2/3) | Added ~8 trades, most were losers → net negative |
| Extended time windows (+1-2 hours) | Added ~3 trades, late-day entries underperform |
| Lower cooldowns (30→15 bars) | Added ~2 trades, minimal impact |
| Lower volume/wick thresholds | Added noise entries → net negative |

**Conclusion:** The original quality gates are well-calibrated. They exist for good reasons — every relaxation we tested made the system worse.

## Why Not $2K/Month Per Account

The user's target was $2,000/month per account. Here's why we're at $1,333 instead:

### The Math

$2,000/month = $91/day. At 5 MNQ ($10/pt per trade):
- Need 9.1 net points per day
- With 0.42 trades/session (our frequency), need $216/trade average
- Current avg winning trade: $292 (enough!)
- But 31% of days are active → need $295/active day

The bottleneck is **trade volume**: only 0.42 trades per session. We need 1+ trade/session at 65%+ WR.

### What Would Get Us There

1. **More data** — 62 sessions is limited. With 200+ sessions, we might validate higher-volume strategies
2. **A 65%+ WR volume strategy** — the EMA strategy at 52.5% is too risky. If we could filter it to 65% WR, it would be gold
3. **Live exit optimization** — manual partial profits at 1:1 R could add $50-100/trade
4. **Wider target testing** — current targets may be leaving money on the table
5. **Intraday strategy switching** — aggressive config for eval, conservative for funded
