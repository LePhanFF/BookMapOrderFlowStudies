## Strategy Definitions

| Label | Setup | Direction | Trigger |
|-------|-------|-----------|---------|
| **80P** | Opens OUTSIDE prior VA, fails, re-enters VA | Opposite to gap | Acceptance candle inside VA |
| **20P** | IB extends >20% of range, confirms with N closes beyond IB | With IB extension | Consecutive closes beyond IB |
| **VA Edge Fade** | Price approaches VA edge, pokes outside, FAILS | Opposite to sweep | Inversion candle / 2x5min / limit |

### How They Relate

```
               PRIOR SESSION VALUE AREA
    ┌──────────────────────────────────────┐
    │                                      │
VAH │──────────────────────────────────────│◄── VA Edge Fade: SHORT when poke above fails
    │              VALUE AREA              │
POC │ · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · · │◄── 80P target / VA Edge Fade target
    │              VALUE AREA              │
VAL │──────────────────────────────────────│◄── VA Edge Fade: LONG when poke below fails
    │                                      │
    └──────────────────────────────────────┘

    80P: Opens HERE (outside) ──► re-enters VA ──► targets opposite side
    20P: IB forms ──► extends beyond IB ──► rides the extension
    VA Edge Fade: Price near edge ──► pokes outside ──► fails ──► fades back in
```
